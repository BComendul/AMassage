Richard Altieri

Personal Trainer and Massage Therapist
Years of Experience
Richard, a certified Massage Therapist, graduated from Southern Connecticut State University. For his undergraduate education he received a Batechelors of Science in Exercise Science. In 20116 he began working as a personal trainer at Apogee Fitness in Hamden, CT. At Apogee he has worked with hundreds of people ranging from post-rehab clients to elite athletes and everywhere in between. During this time period he became an expert in personalized training and nutrition.
Constantly Learning
Rick recently completed a seventeen month program at Connecticut Center for Massage Therapy specializing in deep tissue therapy while also maintaining a full schedule of training clients at Apogee. While at CCMT Rick learned to provide relief for a myriad of pathologies and has since helped clients with everything from cervicogenic headaches to ankle sprains. With massage services ranging from 30-75 minutes in duration, Rick Altieri can provide effective and affordable relief to a variety of musculoskeletal ailments and injuries.
